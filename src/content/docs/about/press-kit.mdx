## Wordmark

The product name is **CephalonEngine** (single word, two capitals). The org is **Cephalon-Labs**. In sentences:

> CephalonEngine is a modular .NET 10 framework.

Avoid:

- "Cephalon engine" (lowercase second word).
- "the Cephalon Engine".
- "Cephalon Engine" (with space).

## One-liner

> CephalonEngine is a modular .NET 10 framework for shipping production-grade modular monoliths, microservices, and event-driven applications from a single composable engine.

## 30-second pitch

> CephalonEngine ships a small, stable engine and a wide catalogue of opt-in companion packages. Adopters compose modules with typed manifests, declared capabilities, and explicit maturity labels — so they know exactly what to rely on. Built on .NET 10, designed for teams that want production rigour without giving up speed.
