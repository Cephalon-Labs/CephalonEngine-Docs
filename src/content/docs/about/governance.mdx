CephalonEngine is currently maintained by Cephalon-Labs with community contributions. Planning, design decisions, and dispute resolution follow a lightweight process.

## Planning cadence

- **Weekly** triage of new issues and PRs.
- **Monthly** roadmap review.
- **Per-minor** planning sync that locks the scope of the next preview release.

## Decision making

- **Default** is consensus among active maintainers.
- **Escalation** for contested decisions goes to a maintainer-only vote with a 72-hour minimum window.
- **Reversal** of a maintainer decision requires a new proposal with new information.

## RFC process

Significant architectural changes follow a lightweight RFC process:

1. Open an Issue with the `kind/rfc` label.
2. Describe motivation, proposed design, alternatives considered, migration impact.
3. Maintainers comment, request revisions.
4. Decision recorded in the Issue and linked from the roadmap.
